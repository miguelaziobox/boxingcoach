# Boxing Coach App

A complete home boxing training app with calendar, timer, and schedule management.

## How to use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. No internet connection required — fully self-contained

## Features

### Calendar
- Monthly training calendar showing your 3-day-a-week schedule
- Tap any day to see the full exercise breakdown
- Mark sessions as done to track progress
- Stats: sessions planned, completed, total hours, streak

### Timer
- Full 60-minute session timer per workout day
- Exercises auto-advance when time runs out
- Boxing rounds play named punch combos with sounds:
  - **Jab** — sharp snappy hit
  - **Cross** — heavy thud
  - **Hook** — deep swing
  - **Uppercut** — rising impact
  - **Body** — low dull thud
  - **Slip** — defensive whoosh
- Punch chips light up in sequence with the name called out
- Bell at end of each exercise, whistle in final 3 seconds

### Schedule
- Pick any 3 days of the week as training days
- Assign Day A (Power), Day B (Speed), Day C (Conditioning) to any day
- Calendar rebuilds instantly after saving

## Workout plan

| Day | Focus | Key work |
|-----|-------|----------|
| A – Power | Strength & heavy bag | 3×3 min bag rounds, push-ups, squats, plank |
| B – Speed | Hand speed & combos | 4×2 min speed work, full combo shadowboxing |
| C – Conditioning | HIIT & body shots | 4×3 min HIIT bursts, body hook rounds |

Each session: warm-up → boxing → strength → core → cool-down = ~60 min

## Files

- `index.html` — app structure
- `style.css` — all styling (light + dark mode)
- `app.js` — all logic (timer, calendar, audio, schedule)
